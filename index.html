Personal Interests

Aside from my academic path, I have always sought to flow art into my life. I believe that your artwork is a mirror reflecting your inner self and what is happening within you. Thus, it not only liberates your mind and imagination but also helps you understand yourself better. That is why I always try to dedicate part of my day to art, be it playing the violin, sketching, sculpting, or simply writing.

One of the greatest pleasures I have experienced in my life through art is playing in a chamber orchestra, which I believe should serve as a model for cooperation and teamwork in all aspects of life. The way each member brings their unique sound and color to the piece truly fascinates me. Every musician shifts roles, supports one another, and contributes to a creation that could never be as beautiful without their individual contributions.

Besides that, I really enjoy trying new recipes and expressing my creativity while cooking. There are endless possibilities to try, and this is what truly amazes me. I also enjoy dancing, especially in groups, as it boosts my energy and the harmony created by our synchronized moves makes the experience even more enjoyable.

Last but not least, I greatly enjoy reading or watching videos about psychology and mental conditions. The human mind is the most sophisticated and flexible machine in this whole universe, and one can never say they know it enough. As it is said, "If the human brain were so simple that we could understand it, we would be so simple that we couldn’t". The more I learn about it, the greater it becomes in my eyes!
